## $\color{mediumpurple}{\text{Abstract}}$

This research explores the intersection of exogenous alkaloid administration and endogenous neurotrophic factor upregulation. Specifically, the capacity of Ibogaine to induce Glial Cell-Derived Neurotrophic Factor (GDNF) is examined as a mechanism for homeostatic "refirmware" of dopaminergic circuits.

## $\color{mediumpurple}{\text{1. Introduction: The Bio-Harmonic Framework}}$

The neural architecture is viewed here not as a static machine, but as a dynamic, resonant system. Using principles of molecular symmetry, the stability of indole-based scaffolds is analyzed in relation to microtubule-mediated signaling.
